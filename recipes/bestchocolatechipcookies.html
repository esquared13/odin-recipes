Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This recipe for chocolate chip cookies is from Dora. The resulting cookies should have crisp edges and chewy middles. The prep time is estimate at twenty minutes, and the cook time is around 10 minutes. In total, the cookies take around an hour to make. The recipe yields 4 dozen, which provides around 24 servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
