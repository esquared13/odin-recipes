Easiest Peanut Butter Fudge

Description

This recipe for peanut butter fudge is from Angieh. She says it is "the best recipe for creamy and delicious peanut butter fudge I have ever used. It is great for sharing at work." In total, the recipe takes around an hour and twenty minutes to make. The prep time is around fifteen minutes, and the cook time is around five minutes. The recipe yields about fifteen servings in an eight-by-eight pan.
